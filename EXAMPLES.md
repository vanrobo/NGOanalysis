## Key Insights from Examples

### 1. **No Single Use - Everything Integrates**

- Event triggers development
- Development informs mentorship
- Mentorship enables events
- Events create impact metrics
- Metrics show career paths
- Career paths motivate engagement

### 2. **AI Makes It Personal**

- Not generic "top 10 careers"
- Specific to _this_ volunteer's profile
- Connected to _their_ interests
- Measured in _their_ impact potential

### 3. **Data-Driven Decision Making**

- See who will succeed in roles (Impact Analyzer)
- Understand why mentors match (Mentorship Matcher)
- Build realistic learning paths (Skill Roadmap)
- Make strategic hiring/retention decisions

### 4. **Scalable Personalization**

- 50 volunteers or 5,000
- Each gets individualized AI recommendations
- At fraction of cost of human career coaches
